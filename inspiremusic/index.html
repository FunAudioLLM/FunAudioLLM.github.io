Overview of InspireMusic

Figure 1. Overview of InspireMusic, which is composed of an audio tokenizer, an autoregressive transformer LM, and a super-resolution flow-matching model. (1) Lower-sampling-rate audio is converted to discrete tokens via an ultra-low-bitrate audio tokenizer. (2) The audio and text tokens are fed to an autoregressive LLM to generate tokens based on next token prediction. (3) The flow-matching model maps the generated tokens to the latent features with high-resolution fine-grained acoustic details obtained via HiFi-Codec from higher-sampling-rate audio, to ensure the acoustic information flow connected with high fidelity through models. (4) The vocoder decoder produces high-quality 48kHz audio waveform.
